How to use

  1. Open Search Networks and select Species, Source, and Tissue. Click Apply.
  2. Review the left panel Summary. Use top-right buttons to Download GRN or open Source Data.
  3. Inspect the right panel Cell types, showing RNA/ATAC counts per cell type (with totals and a small chart).
